    1. Accuracy Humans are prone to error, even if we provide them with supporting computational devices. Automated attendance managemant systems ensure accurate time records and minimize the inevitable and costly errors with manual data entry. It takes time to process paper sheets and time cards, create schedules, authorize leave and overtime, and create payroll manually. Free up precious administration time with an automated system that does it all for you, from keeping track of employee hours to automatically importing imformation into your payroll system. The time and effort saved combined data accuracy helps in optomizing the use of resources which lead to increased productivity and improves profits.
